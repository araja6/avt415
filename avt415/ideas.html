Three Project Ideas

Recipe Sharing Platform
This would be a blog style website, or social media style mobile phone applilcation that would allow users to create profiles, share images, videos and written posts about their own recipes.
examples: Medium, tumblr, vsco
Virtual Closet
This would be a website or mobile phone application that would allow users to create a collection of items that they have in their closet, either by taking photos of their clothing, or searching the web for the items they own. This would be used as an efficient way to create outfits and save time.
examples: Virtual closet from the movie Clueless, Polyvore
Plant Health Tracker
This mobile phone application will help users keep track of when to water their plants, provide information on how often to water them and how much water to give them. It would also include other suggestions such as plant food, preferred sunlight, everything regarding plant health.
The app would also be useful for gardening by providing information and tips on how to take care of and grown certain vegetables/plants/flowers while also keeping track of harvest time and the growth process of plants in the garden.
Sends out reminders to users when its time to water their plants or garden.
examples: Plant Nanny, period tracker apps
